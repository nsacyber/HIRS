# About HIRS

HIRS is a proof-of-concept system designed to spur the adoption of cryptographic validation 
checks for corporate computing devices. The HIRS team is composed of a group of software developers 
and hardware engineers. Although HIRS is proof-of-concept, the team has performed pilot runs with 
organizations to demonstrate the capabilities and promote adoption.
